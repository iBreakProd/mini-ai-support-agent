# Shipping Policy — Arctic (India)

**Processing time**
- Orders ship within 1–2 business days (Mon–Sat).
- Same-day processing cutoff: 2:00 PM IST from the nearest fulfillment node.

**Shipping methods & typical timelines (India)**
- Metro to metro: 2–4 business days.
- Metro to non-metro: 3–6 business days.
- Remote/low-connectivity PIN codes: 5–10 business days.
- No international shipping; India-only delivery.

**Rates**
- Orders ₹1,500+ ship free (standard).
- Orders below ₹1,500: shipping is carrier/zone calculated at checkout.
- Express options (where available) are shown at checkout with live pricing.

**Cash on delivery (COD)**
- COD available on select PIN codes; carrier COD fees may apply.
- Repeated COD refusals may lead to COD being disabled for the account/PIN.

**Delivery attempts**
- 2–3 delivery attempts depending on carrier. After failed attempts, the shipment may be returned to origin (RTO). Reshipping may incur additional charges if failure was customer-caused.

**Tracking**
- Tracking is sent by email/SMS/WhatsApp (where enabled) when the label is created.
- Tracking events can take up to 12 hours to reflect carrier scans.

**Address accuracy**
- Please ensure correct PIN code, phone number, and full address. Address changes after dispatch may not be possible and can delay delivery.

**Damaged, leaking, or missing items**
- If packaging is damaged or items are leaking/missing, photograph the outer box, inner packaging, and product labels within 72 hours of delivery and contact support. We will reship or refund as appropriate.

**Heat sensitivity**
- Store away from direct sunlight/heat on receipt. If product temperature abuse is suspected on delivery, photograph immediately and contact support.

**Contact**
- Email: support@arcticwater.com
- Support hours: Mon–Fri, 9:00 AM–6:00 PM IST
