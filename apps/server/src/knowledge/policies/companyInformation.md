# Company Information — Arctic (India)
- Arctic is an India-based spring water brand focused on still, sparkling, and infused water products with balanced minerals and clean taste.

**Who we are**
- We source from verified spring sites in India with naturally balanced minerals.
- We operate bottled-water and infused-water lines with strict safety and purity controls.

**What we make**
- Still spring water (multiple sizes)
- Sparkling spring water
- Lightly infused variants (e.g., citrus zest, berry hint)
- Mineral-balanced electrolyte blends for hydration

**Sourcing & quality**
- Each batch is tested for purity, mineral profile, and contaminants.
- Bottled in BPA-free, recyclable materials; low-additive processing to preserve taste.
- Compliant with applicable BIS/FSSAI norms for packaged drinking water.

**Sustainability**
- Recyclable packaging with an increasing share of post-consumer recycled content.
- Route optimization and hub placement to reduce transport footprint within India.
