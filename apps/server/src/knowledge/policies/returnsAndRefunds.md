# Returns & Refunds — Arctic (India)

**Return window**
- Unopened shelf-stable products: 30 days from delivery.
- Opened or partially used bottles: not eligible (food safety and tamper concerns).
- Promotional/limited editions: final sale unless damaged or defective on arrival.

**How to start a return**
- Email support@arcticwater.com with order ID, item(s), reason, and photos if damaged.
- We issue an RMA and return instructions. Unauthorized returns may be refused.

**Condition requirements**
- Items must be sealed, in original packaging, and include any freebies/promo items.
- Visible tampering, broken seals, or missing items may void eligibility.

**Refunds**
- Processed after inspection: typically 5–7 business days to cards/UPI; 7–10 for some banks.
- COD orders: refunded via UPI/bank transfer (we will request details securely).
- Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or damage.

**Exchanges**
- For flavor/size changes, return (if eligible) and place a new order. Direct swaps are not guaranteed.

**Damaged/defective on arrival**
- Send photos of the outer box, inner packing, labels, and the affected items within 72 hours of delivery. We will replace or refund based on stock availability.

**Return shipping**
- If we erred or product is defective/damaged: we cover return or reship at no cost.
- Preference changes/ordering mistakes: customer covers return shipping; original shipping is not refunded.

**RTO (return to origin) / refused deliveries**
- If a shipment is refused or undeliverable after attempts, it may be returned to origin. Reshipment may incur additional charges. For prepaid orders, refunds (minus shipping/fees) are processed after we receive and inspect the return.
